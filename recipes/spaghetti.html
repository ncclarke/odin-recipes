Spaghetti

Description:

An authentic tasty spaghetti recipe that can't be beat


Ingredients:

  • 12 oz (340g) spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook until al dente (about 8–10 minutes). Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir and let simmer for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce and toss until well coated.
  5. Plate the spaghetti and top with fresh herbs and grated Parmesan if desired.
